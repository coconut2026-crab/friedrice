# Data Privacy Policy

## Overview

CIPHER is committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains how the application handles (or rather, does not handle) your data.

## Data Collection

**CIPHER collects no data whatsoever.**

- ❌ No personal information
- ❌ No images or files
- ❌ No passwords or keys
- ❌ No usage analytics or telemetry
- ❌ No cookies or local storage
- ❌ No third-party tracking scripts

## Data Processing

All image encryption and decryption is performed **locally in your browser**. Your files and passwords never leave your device.

- Images are read into browser memory, encrypted, and discarded.
- Passwords are used to derive encryption keys and are not persisted.
- Encrypted output files are generated in-browser and saved directly to your device.

## Third-Party Services

CIPHER does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms. The application is a self-contained static website.

## Data Storage

CIPHER does not store any data on any server. The application has no backend, no database, and no cloud storage.

## Your Rights

Since CIPHER collects no data, there is nothing to access, modify, export, or delete. You have full control over your files at all times — they exist only on your device.

## Children's Privacy

CIPHER does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be reflected in this document and committed to the project repository.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com).
